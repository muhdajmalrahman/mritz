MI Store

Smart automated stores powered by artificial intelligence with a system to track the moment of customers, eliminating payment queues and providing customers with a hassle-free shopping experience. Undoubtedly.

The MI Store will be a holistic retail solution for all circumstances ranging from precise analytics for footfall counting to brand security and standardization. We offer businesses with one-stop market leading AI solutions.

MI Studio

Our innovative spirit pushes us to go above and beyond. AR and VR are changing the way children learn. MI Studio allows students to immerse themselves in learning by allowing them to explore 3D models and retain more information since their brains assimilate data through a variety of senses such as sight, sound, and touch.

This is not just an AR VR education system; it is a tech build studio with enormous innovations in knowledge transmission.
